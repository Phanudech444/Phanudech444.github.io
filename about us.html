About Us

Founded in 2011. We are a Bangkok-based design
firm who create efficient architecture,
interior and landscape design with style and enthusiastic mind.

Charcoal Design will be stand by your side since the very first day of the project until the day of success.
The scope of services include,
- Architectural Design
- Interior Design
- Landscape Design
- Signage System Design
- Corporate Identity, Brand Identity
- Engineering Design
And we are very proud to present a new project under Charcoal Design called Canvas Studio. It is a place to rent for photography, meetings, or activities that require private space.
